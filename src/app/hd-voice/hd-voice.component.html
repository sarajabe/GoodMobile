HD Voice

A new dimension in voice quality for Good Mobile customers

Get clear audio on calls

HD Voice is a wideband audio technology that helps make calls sound much crisper and allows you to use multiple features on your phone at the same time. By extending the frequency range of audio signals, HD Voice does two things well.

Multitask with VoLTE

HD Voice allows you to do it all once on your phone: Talk, browse, post, message, use apps and stream at 4G LTE speeds.

How does HD Voice work?

HD Voice, which is powered by VoLTE (voice over LTE), uses wide-band audio technology and noise cancellation. That means voices sound more natural, and you’ll hear much less background noise compared to standard voice calls
