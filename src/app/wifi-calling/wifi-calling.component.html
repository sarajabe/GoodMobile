Wi-Fi Calling

Talk in more places

Beat Poor Cellular Reception with Wi-Fi Calling

Wi-Fi Calling lets you talk from indoor locations where it’s hard even for a strong cellular signal to reach (skyscraper buildings, the basement, areas that have a lot of metal in the walls, areas with heavy foliage, etc.)

With Wi-Fi Calling, you can talk on your mobile phone as you do on the cellular network. (Your phone must be HD Voice compatible and VoLTE must be enabled to use Wi-Fi Calling).

Call and Text with Wi-Fi

  • Wi-Fi Calling is easy to set up and use and allows you to make and receive calls and texts over a wireless Internet connection versus the cell network.

  • Once enabled, Wi-Fi Calling works automatically – no new number or app required.

  • Calls to U.S. numbers via Wi-Fi from anywhere in the world can be made at no additional charge.

  • Calls to international numbers from the U.S. are billed at your international long-distance rates. Good Mobile International Calling.

  • Calls to premium numbers such as 411 are billed at standard premium rates.

Stay Connected While Traveling Internationally

Wi-Fi Calling is especially handy while traveling outside of the US so you don’t pay international long distance or roaming. Please note Wi-Fi Calling is restricted in some countries.

Restrictions apply – see Wi-Fi Calling Frequently Asked Questions

Good Mobile Wi-Fi Calling: Requires a Wi-Fi Calling capable smartphone and accessible Wi-Fi network. Once enabled, phone automatically makes/receives calls/text messages over Wi-Fi in the U.S., Puerto Rico, and the U.S. Virgin Islands (“Domestic Coverage Area”) if wireless network coverage is weak or unavailable, or outside the Domestic Coverage Area anytime you connect to a Wi-Fi network. Wi-Fi Calling is available on select devices.

Charges: (Voice) In the U.S. or internationally, you can use Wi-Fi Calling to call numbers in the U.S. (the “Domestic Coverage Area” or “DCA”) at no additional charge (excluding 411 calls and other premium numbers). International long distance rates apply when calling international numbers from the DCA. International roaming rates apply when calling international numbers while traveling outside the DCA.

Go to available rates for international rates and options. (SMS/MMS) Text messages sent/received through Wi-Fi Calling count against the messaging usage limits of your plan.

Service limits: Use of Wi-Fi Calling may be restricted in some countries. See excluded countries in the FAQ.

If you move in or out of Wi-Fi coverage while on a Wi-Fi call, your call will disconnect unless you have HD Voice coverage (911 calls will disconnect even if you are within HD Voice coverage). HD Voice not available everywhere.

To enable Wi-Fi Calling, must have a wireless account provisioned for HD Voice.Visit goodmobile.org/hd-voice.com or our FAQs for more details.
