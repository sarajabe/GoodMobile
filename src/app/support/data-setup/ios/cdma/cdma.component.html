Once you restart or power on your phone, the activation should begin automatically. If not, please proceed to the steps below*:

Start by connecting to a Wi-Fi network

  • Go to the phone app and tap Keypad.
  • Enter ##25327# and tap Call.
  • When the “Resetting Subscriber Settings” message appears, tap Ok/Dismiss.

    If the “Waiting for Activation” message appears, tap Ok/Dismiss. Otherwise, wait 5-10 seconds for the word “Searching” to appear in the top left-hand corner.

  • When activation is successful, a message may appear or the phone will show signal signs again.

Next up, go to the phone app and tap Keypad again

  • Enter ##873283# and tap Call.
  • A “Starting Service Update” message appears, tap Ok/Dismiss.
  • If the “Service Update Complete” message appears, tap Ok/Dismiss.
  • Your phone is now ready for use.

*Don’t worry—these steps will not erase any personal photos, videos, contact info or other files from your iPhone.
