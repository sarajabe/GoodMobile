Sprint network LTE instructions

PROFILE UPDATE – ANDROID

Please follow these instructions to complete data set up on your Android device:

1. Power phone on, and make sure you are not connected to wifi.

2. Look in your settings for Update Profile. Some possible menu configurations on Android are:

  • Settings -> About phone -> System Updates -> Update Profile
  • Settings -> About Phone -> Update Profile

3. Once you’ve selected to update the profile, all you need to do is wait until the update completes and then reboot your phone.

If you have a problem completing the update, make sure that Wi-FI is turned off, and that you’re in an area with good coverage and a strong data signal.

REMOVE YOUR PHONE’S OLD CARRIER INFORMATION

Go to the dial pad or phone app:

1. Enter # # 72786 # into the dial pad. Don’t tap the call icon or try to connect.

2. If prompted, enter your MSL.

3. Confirm the reset.

Allow the phone to restart and go through the activation process.

Note: Many LTE phones can perform a carrier reset over a Wi-Fi connection, but some phones cannot update over Wi-Fi. If the steps above do not complete on your phone, disable Wi-Fi and enable data and try again.

OLDER PHONES, MANUAL PROGRAMMING INSTRUCTION

1. Power the phone on.

2. Enter your MSL code like this: ##MSL# (If your MSL code is 123456, you would enter # # 123456#

3. Click on Edit

4. Type in your Phone Number (MDN)

5. Click ‘OK’

6. Type in your MSID (given at activation, or you can find this on your MyAccount page

7. Click ‘OK’

8. Click ‘Done’

9. The Phone will power cycle.

10. The Phone is now successfully programmed!
