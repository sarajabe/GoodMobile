ANDROID SMARTPHONE LTE INSTRUCTIONS:

Android version 4.0 (Ice Cream Sandwich) and up

First things first.....

If you just activated your phone, please make sure to turn it off and back on. This ensures your phone will get its first set of programming direct from the towers, and will force your phone to register quickly.

Secondly...

Important data settings need to be programmed into your phone. This is like the ‘key to the data portion of the network. Without this, your mobile data will not work.

The following instructions are for most Android devices. While your phone’s menu may look different, the information which goes into each of the fields is the same, no matter what model device you have. No data connection is necessary when entering these settings but a data-capable device is required to surf the web and watch videos online.

Phones which are 5G capable require special data settings to be able to use the 5G network. You must also turn on 5G through your account summary page.
